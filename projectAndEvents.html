Projects and Events

Es posible

Is an event organized by TU FUTURO and offers a workshop for juniors and seniors about difficulties they may encounter during in college or future work.

Exito Latino

It is an event organized by TU FUTURO and offers a workshop for 7th to sophomores that talks about High School and possibilities.

Fiesta Latina

Fiesta Latina is a fair where there are many different Latino cultures and where our club and many other organizations prepare food and activities. Many people work at the Fiesta Latina to earn some money.

Latino Formal

Latino Formal is a Formal that started in 2023 as a Senior Project. Although it is not a project-specific of the LOVE club, we always help the students who want to organize this prom as their senior project

Scolarships

Our club is currently working to organize fundraising throughout the year to get enough money for a scholarship.

How do I know if I am eligible?

To be eligible for our scholarship, you must constantly participate in our events and show interest in volunteering and education! Join us now!
