Welcome to L.O.V.E Club

Founded in 2009, L.O.V.E. (Latino-American Organization of Volunteers in Education) is dedicated to empowering Latino students in their pursuit of higher education. Our mission is to create opportunities, foster connections, and promote academic excellence.

Our East High School Chapter

At Columbus East High School, our L.O.V.E club chapter is a vibrant and growing community. We're committed to:

  • Providing educational support
  • Organizing cultural events
  • Creating networking opportunities
  • Fostering leadership skills

Get Involved

Join our leadership board and make a lasting impact on Latino students' lives. We offer numerous opportunities for growth and community service.

Be part of something big - help with volunteering and spread kindness through education!
